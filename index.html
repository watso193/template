Trevors Story

Trevor’s narrative was more than just a written story.
He was able to pull together different sources of his memory together to form a video.
He wanted to share with people how a person that he looked up to tat suffered from the same things as him went about dealing with his problems.
Trevor made it clear that what his uncle did was wrong in his eyes and that that sets an example for what people that suffer form the same problems should do.
Trevor’s video was intriguing because of the way he presented things. He used things that he had written when he was younger, and put images of writing in the video to create a more personal effect on the person watching the video. It tries to put people in his shoes so that they feel like they are actually reading his original writings. When Trevor presented his message he seemed like he was trying to catch the attention of the viewer and once he knew he had the attention then he would explain the issue. The biggest thing I took form this video to help change my own Lived Experience is how personal it was. Trevor was explaining exactly how he felt with everything that had to do with him being gay and his uncle.

Social Network analysis

Technology has completely changed the way the writing and communication happen. The more advanced technology becomes the easier it is for people to communicate to one another. People no longer have to talk to people face to face. They can call, text, email and come in contact with each other in numerous other ways as well. I personally think people have became more cowardly and are not confrontational because it is so easy to say what you want without people being in front of you. Twitter, Facebook, blogs, and the Internet allow people to say whatever they want with out having to worry what would happen to them if they said it out loud. People don’t use proper English on any of the social networks either because it sounds “cool.” When I first started chatting with my friends on AOL I would use any slang possible. It felt like I was speaking a different language that only people my age could understand. I would catch myself using the same type of language in school and in writing papers. People no longer use the correct way of writing unless they absolutely have to. It feels weird to read something that actually makes sense..

CHRYSLER

The Chrysler commercial that takes place in Detroit takes on a different approach than most commercials. Rather than making the commercial flashy with bright lights and colors Chrysler went for a more meaningful message. The commercial has an older man speaking that sounds like he has not had the easiest life based on his raspy voice. The clips of Detroit are gloomy which show the heartache and suffering that the city has experienced. As the clips keep rolling the voice explains some of the things that have happened to Detroit along with some of the things that people from around the country portray of the city. People act as if nothing good can come from Detroit. The tagline “Imported from Detroit” means that Detroit is looked at as a foreign place, place that people do not want to be a part of nor believe in. Chrysler uses the word “imported” because it shows that Chrysler is not only something of Detroit. It is an American company. Detroit sets an example of what the rest of the country is capable of.


Codecademy

Codecademy has been one of the most complicated assignments I have ever had to complete. I am terrible when it comes to operating computers. I can barely use Microsoft Word let alone create structures for websites and webpages. Before Codecademy I had no idea what HTML or CSS meant. I had no idea what coding even meant. To be completely honest I am glad that I had to learn it. I think it is interesting to see how websites are created. HTML is almost like a completely different language. There a countless codes that need to be memorized to become fluent with HTML. I use the word fluent because it reminds me of learning a language. When you learn a language you learn the basics. New material is the introduced and you practice that until it becomes easy. Once spate material is learned the materials can be brought together and reviewed. Codecademy taught HTML and CSS in a similar way. I don’t know exactly if I will ever have to use HTML, I most likely will, but learning the basics now will help me in the future.
